# Chapter 1 : Computer System

## Introduction

The word "computer" comes from the Latin word **"computare"** which means to calculate. So a computer is normally considered to be a calculating device that can perform arithmetic operations at fast speed.

## Definition

In simple terms, any electronic machine that can perform **IPOS (Input Process Output Storage)** cycle is known as computer.

An electronic device which accept raw data, process the data, stores the data for future use and gives the desired information (result) as output, such a device which have memory is called computer.

---

## Characteristics of Computer

Following are some of the features of a digital computer:

**1. Speed** — Computer can calculate very complex data at a very fast rate that human being may not be able to do. The speed of the computer is measured in terms of fraction of second.

**2. Diligence** — It Means, is absolutely free from physical tiredness and mental fatigue. Because a computer has maximum capacity to perform any kind of tasks repeatedly at the same level of timing periods.

**3. Versatile** — Computers have multi-purpose capacities. A computer can do any types of jobs at the same time. That's why computers are versatile devices. At the same level of period of timings, it provides loading an environment to run many of tasks as we needed.

**4. Reliability** — It is the most recent demand and means in real life of all human beings. Men are imperfect but it has capable to make copy from them. So the modern age is depending onto it because of its capabilities a lot.

**5. Automatic** — Computer is an automatic machine. Everything that is given to computer are processed and done by computer automatically according to the instructions provided.

**6. Accuracy** — The result of the computer is 100% accurate if the input data are correct.

**7. Storage Capacity** — Computers have data storage devices. It stores the data permanently or temporarily. There are mainly two types of storage devices (Primary and Secondary). The capacity of the storage is measured in terms of bytes. The small unit is bit (0 or 1). Computers stores the data form of bit.

**8. Word Length** — A computer can process 16 bits, 32 bits and 64 bits data at a time. The number of bits the computer can process at a time known as word length.

### On The Basis of Operation

**Analog Computer** — The computer which can process analog quantities (continuous data) is called an analog computer. It is specially designed to compute physical forces such as temperature, pressure etc. Presley is an example of analog computer.

**Digital Computer** — The computer which works on discrete data (discontinuous data, binary system or 0 or 1) is known as digital computer. For Example, IBM PC, IBM compatible, Apple/Mac. computer etc.

**Hybrid Computer** — A Computer, which has combined features of both analog and digital, is known as hybrid computer. It can convert data from analog to digital and digital to analog. These computers are used for scientific research and hospitals.

## Hybrid Computer

A Computer, which has combined features of both analog and digital, is known as hybrid computer. It can convert data from analog to digital and digital to analog. These computers are used for scientific research and hospitals.

### Characteristics of Hybrid Computer

- It is a combination of qualities of analog as well as digital computers.
- It can process both continuous and discrete data.
- It has capability to convert one type of data into another.
- It is the special purpose machine.
- It is mostly used in airplane, hospital, weather forecasting.
- It usually has high cost compared to analog and digital computer.
